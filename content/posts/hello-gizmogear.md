# Welcome to GizmoGear

We're officially live. 🎉

GizmoGear is a clean, professional tech blog with a Zambian lens — covering gadgets, digital services, developer tools, and the trends shaping how we live and work online.

## What to Expect

- **Honest gadget reviews** — no fluff, real use cases
- **Developer content** — tools, frameworks, and workflow tips
- **Service deep-dives** — what's worth your money and what isn't
- **The Zambian angle** — locally relevant, globally informed

## Why Zambia?

Because there's a gap. Global tech content rarely speaks to how things actually work on the ground here — bandwidth constraints, local pricing, regional availability. We'll bridge that.
